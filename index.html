About Me

I was born in Manipal, India, and raised in Kochi, two small places that shaped a big part of who I am today and made me grounded, curious, and full of energy. Growing up, I was the happy to go kid who danced nonstop, and my parents still joke that I never really took my first steps, I went straight into dancing. I would play music and move for hours, and 24 years later, that love for dance is still my constant, a reminder of joy, rhythm, and showing up fully as myself. Alongside that creative side, I have always had a keen eye for patterns. My favorite thing as a child was solving puzzles, and as I grew older, that same instinct naturally turned into a fascination with numbers, logic, and the stories hidden inside data. Today, I am excited by the challenge of making complexity feel clear, and going forward, I want to keep doing what I have always loved, working with numbers and patterns, but in a way that creates real impact by turning data into direction and using insight to do some good in the world.

How I fell in love with Analytics

My love for analytics and data science actually came from my brother. I used to listen to him get super excited about how data is everywhere ,like literally all around us and how we all use it in some way every day. It kind of stuck with me and made me want to explore it myself. As I started my professional journey, I quickly realized that the decisions that really make a difference in business aren’t just about intuition—they’re about having a solid understanding of the numbers behind them. I found myself fascinated by how data could tell a story, highlight opportunities, and even prevent mistakes before they happen. Seeing the impact of insights applied in real-world scenarios made me appreciate just how powerful analytics can be, and it motivated me to dive deeper into learning how to use data thoughtfully and effectively.

What lies ahead?

I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Business Analytics at the University of Southern California, and every day here has been an opportunity to explore my love for data and problem-solving. From attending workshops to participating in events, I’ve been able to challenge myself and gain experiences that are shaping the way I approach analytics in the real world. Through this journey, I’ve discovered that my true interests lie in the healthcare, entertainment, and CPG industries, and I’m excited about the possibility of contributing as an analyst in one of them after graduation. Right now, I’m looking for a summer 2026 internship in analytics, a role where I can continue learning, applying my skills, and helping solve meaningful problems. If you think I might be a fit for a role, I’d love to connect you can reach out to me through the “Contact Me” page.
